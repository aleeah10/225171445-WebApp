About Us

The Disease Tracker Website is a powerful tool designed specifically for hospitals to efficiently monitor and manage patient health data. This intuitive platform allows healthcare providers to input and track the number and percentage of patients diagnosed with various health conditions. It facilitates the tracking of diseases within each ward or department, including OB-Gyne, Medical, Surgical, and Pediatric units.

MISSION

To empower healthcare providers with data-driven insights to effectively manage and prevent the spread of infectious diseases.

VISION

To be the leading provider of disease tracking solutions, revolutionizing healthcare management and improving patient outcomes.
